01 // POSITIONING

Applied AI that holds up under production constraints.

I design systems that do more than wrap an LLM. They ingest messy inputs, enforce structure, preserve evidence, and stay operable when accuracy, cost, and reliability actually matter.

Most AI systems break under real-world conditions — unreliable inputs, unclear structure, and lack of verification. I focus on building systems that remain stable under those constraints, using explicit schemas, verification layers, and operational guardrails.

That work spans workflow design, enrichment pipelines, data modeling, verification layers, caching strategy, observability, and the interfaces analysts rely on to make decisions.
